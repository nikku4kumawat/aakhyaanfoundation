Sampoorn Shiksha

Sampoorn Shiksha is an educational initiative dedicated to improving learning outcomes and the holistic development of underprivileged children. Through remedial education, value-based learning, community participation and teacher capacity building, the programme empowers every child with knowledge, confidence and life skills for a brighter future.

Quality Education

Providing equitable access to meaningful learning opportunities.

Teacher Training

Enhancing instructors' skills for quality education.

Value Education

Building ethics, confidence and moral values.

Holistic Development

Developing knowledge, creativity and life skills.

Our Approach

Project Goal

Enhancing educational outcomes and holistic development of underprivileged children through quality and value-based education.

Project Purpose

Bridging learning gaps through remedial classes, teacher training, community engagement and value-based education.

Project Outputs

Improved enrolment, better academic performance, trained instructors, stronger community participation and enhanced moral values.

Youth Voice & Participation

Encouraging youth leadership, gender equality, safer communities and skill development opportunities.
